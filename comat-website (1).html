OUR STORY

Founded in Rwanda

THE CO-MAT STORY

CO-MAT was born from a simple but powerful observation: Rwanda's construction sector was growing rapidly, yet builders struggled to find reliable, quality materials and trusted suppliers in one place.

Founded in Rulindo District, Northern Province, we set out to bridge that gap — creating Rwanda's premier construction materials advertising platform, connecting suppliers with builders across the country.

Today, CO-MAT is a trusted name in construction supply chains, helping thousands of projects come to life with the right materials at the right time. We are proud to be part of Rwanda's infrastructure growth story.

Our journey has been driven by integrity, quality, and a passion for building — not just structures, but lasting partnerships with our clients and the communities we serve.

OUR SERVICES

Construction Material Advertising

CONNECTING BUILDERS
WITH MATERIALS

CO-MAT specializes in advertising construction materials — giving suppliers a powerful platform to reach Rwanda's booming construction market, while helping buyers discover quality, verified products they can trust. From foundations to finishing, we advertise it all.

Structural Materials Advertising

We advertise cement, reinforced steel, iron sheets, bricks, concrete blocks, and all foundation-to-frame structural materials — connecting suppliers directly with contractors who need them most.

🪵

Finishing Materials Advertising

Tiles, paints, flooring, ceiling boards, glass, plumbing fixtures and interior/exterior finishing products — our platform ensures buyers find exactly the finish they're looking for.

🔧

Hardware & Tools Advertising

From hand tools to heavy equipment, CO-MAT advertises construction hardware and tools across Rwanda, bridging tool suppliers with contractors and builders at every scale.

Road & Infrastructure Materials

Aggregates, asphalt, road signage, drainage pipes — we advertise materials for road construction and public infrastructure projects across Rwanda's provinces.

Roofing Materials Advertising

Iron sheets, roof trusses, waterproofing membranes, gutters and roofing accessories — all advertised on our platform with verified supplier contacts ready to serve your project.

Electrical & Plumbing Materials

Wiring, conduits, pipes, fittings, sanitary ware and MEP materials — CO-MAT ensures buyers and suppliers in the mechanical and electrical trade connect efficiently.
